## Sensitivity — leave-one-out

Pooled RR stable across all 13 omissions (range 0.452–0.533); every leave-one-out 95% CI

## Interpretation (for Results section)

BCG vaccination was associated with a 51% relative reduction in tuberculosis risk
(pooled RR 0.489, 95% CI 0.344–0.696). Heterogeneity was very high (I² = 92.2%,
τ² = 0.313, Q = 152.23, p < 0.001), and the 95% prediction interval (0.155–1.549)
crosses 1.0, indicating that a future trial setting could plausibly show no protective
effect — consistent with the known latitude-dependent efficacy of BCG.
